# Post-launch 90-day Growth Plan

## Days 1-7
- Ship 15 cornerstone articles from calendar; manually QA E-E-A-T.
- Submit sitemap to GSC; request indexing top 10 URLs.
- Enable email capture; launch double opt-in welcome series.

## Days 8-30
- Publish 5-7 articles/week; run internal linking automation after each publish.
- A/B test hero CTAs (signup vs affiliate) via KV flag; run 500 sessions per variant.
- Launch 2 lead magnets (PDF checklist, mini-guide) using digital product pages.
- Start building 10 contextual backlinks via HARO/Help a B2B Writer.

## Days 31-60
- Layer video shorts for top 10 posts; embed YouTube.
- Apply to Ezoic when 10k sessions; shift to Mediavine at 50k.
- Begin monthly revenue tracking in admin; set targets.
- Refresh top 5 posts with FAQ schema based on new queries.

## Days 61-90
- Expand to 2 subcategories; add 20 longtails; refresh internal links.
- Negotiate 3 sponsored placements; set rate card in affiliate manager.
- Roll weekly digest; include top posts + one promo.
- Monitor Analytics Engine for top exit pages; add contextual CTA blocks.

Automation checkpoints: cron ensures calendar always 90 days ahead; queue generates drafts when scheduled; KV-controlled A/B stays within 1/500 density; email milestones fire at subscriber and traffic thresholds.
